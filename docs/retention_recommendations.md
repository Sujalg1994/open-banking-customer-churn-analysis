# Retention Recommendations

## Executive Summary

The churn scoring workflow identifies customers with weakening banking relationships, low product depth, declining salary inflows, reduced digital engagement, complaints, or elevated overdraft usage. The output supports a targeted retention queue rather than broad campaign activity.

## Recommended Actions

- Use service recovery calls for customers with recent complaints.
- Offer salary-switch or current-account incentives where salary inflow has stopped or declined.
- Use product deepening offers for one-product customers.
- Trigger digital engagement campaigns for customers with very low app usage or falling digital activity.
- Offer financial wellbeing support where overdraft usage is high.

## BA Notes

- Campaign eligibility rules should be agreed with product, compliance, and customer treatment teams.
- Customer contact history should be added before operational deployment to avoid over-contact.
- Retention outcomes should be measured through test/control tracking.
