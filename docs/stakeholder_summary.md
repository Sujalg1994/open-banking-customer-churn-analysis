# Stakeholder Summary

## Decision Supported

Prioritise customers for retention interventions based on churn probability and commercial value.

## Executive View

The churn score separates customers into high, medium, and low-risk bands. High-risk customers show a materially higher churn rate and should be reviewed first, especially where average balance, salary inflow, or customer complaints indicate commercial value or service risk.

## Recommended KPIs

- Churn rate
- High-risk customer count
- Value at risk
- Salary inflow loss risk
- Active digital engagement
- Product holdings per customer
- Complaint rate
- Average balance movement

## Example Actions

- Contact high-value customers with falling engagement.
- Review complaint handling for customers with high churn probability.
- Offer product bundles to customers with low product depth.
- Use salary-switch incentives when salary inflow has stopped or declined.
- Track campaign performance with control groups before scaling.
