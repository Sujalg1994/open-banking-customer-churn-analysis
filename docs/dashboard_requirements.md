# Dashboard Requirements

## Audience

Customer insight, retail banking product owners, retention teams, and business analysis stakeholders.

## Dashboard Pages

1. Churn Overview
2. Segment and Region Analysis
3. Retention Priority Queue
4. Recommended Actions

## KPIs

- Customer count
- Churn rate
- High-risk customer count
- Value at risk
- Average churn score
- Salary inflow loss indicator
- Complaint rate
- Low digital engagement rate

## Required Slicers

- Customer segment
- Region
- Churn risk band
- Retention action
- Salary inflow flag
- Product count

## Acceptance Criteria

- Risk-band counts reconcile to the scored customer dataset.
- Priority queue includes medium and high churn risk customers.
- Value at risk is calculated consistently across dashboard pages.
- No real customer data is used.
